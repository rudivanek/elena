# Elena — What This App Does
## We'd Love Your Honest Opinion

**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-20
**Status:** Early-stage app — looking for real feedback before launch

---

## 1. What Elena Is

Elena is an app you can talk to when you are feeling something and need a space to process it. It is not a therapist, a chatbot, or a mood tracker. It is closer to a private journal that talks back — one that gets to know you over time.

The idea is simple: most of us have moments where we feel overwhelmed, anxious, or stuck, and we do not always have someone to talk to right away. Elena is designed to be available in those moments — calm, private, and without judgment.

Over time, Elena pays attention to what you share. Not to analyze you, but to notice patterns and reflect them back to you: "You've mentioned work stress several times this week" or "Your mood seemed better after you wrote in your journal." The goal is that over weeks of use, you start to understand yourself a little better.

Elena is not a replacement for professional mental health support. If you are in crisis or need clinical help, Elena will point you toward the right resources.

---

## 2. What You Can Do With It

- Talk to Elena whenever you feel overwhelmed, anxious, stuck, or just need to express something
- Write in a private, encrypted journal that only you can access
- Get guided reflection prompts when you open your journal — questions that help you go a little deeper
- Turn a chat conversation into a journal entry if it felt meaningful
- See a weekly summary of your emotional patterns — which topics came up most, which days felt harder
- Get gentle nudges from Elena when she has noticed something worth sharing
- Choose from suggested conversation starters if you do not know how to begin

---

## 3. What Makes It Different

Most apps in this space do one thing. Chat apps just chat. Journal apps just store text. Mood trackers just count entries.

Elena tries to connect all three.

When you talk to Elena, those conversations feed into your weekly insights. When you write in your journal, those themes also appear in your patterns. Elena's memory of what you have shared shapes how she responds in future conversations — so over time, it should feel less like starting from scratch every time.

The goal is that each part of the app makes the other parts more useful. Your journal makes your insights richer. Your insights make Elena's responses more relevant. Your conversations give you something worth reflecting on in your journal.

Whether that actually works in practice is part of what we want your feedback on.

---

## 4. How It Works — Simple Flow

1. You open the app and start a conversation with Elena, or open your journal
2. You write what is on your mind — there is no right or wrong way to start
3. Elena responds with something thoughtful, and offers a few suggestions if you want to go deeper
4. You can continue the conversation, save it to your journal, or just close the app
5. After a week of use, an Insights page starts to fill in — showing your mood patterns, streaks, and a short written summary
6. When you come back after a few days away, Elena may reference something from your recent history to make the conversation feel continuous rather than starting over

That is the full loop. Nothing requires you to manually rate your mood or fill out forms. It all happens through natural conversation and journaling.

---

## 5. What You Might Get Out of It

**Clarity.** Sometimes just writing something down or having a conversation about it helps you understand what you actually feel. Elena is designed to make that easier.

**Feeling heard.** Elena is not going to tell you what to do or give you a list of coping strategies. The goal is to respond in a way that makes you feel understood first.

**Patterns you would not otherwise notice.** It is hard to see your own emotional habits week to week. The insight summaries are designed to surface things like "you tend to feel worse on Sundays" or "this topic keeps coming up" in plain language — not in charts or numbers.

**A private space.** All your conversations and journal entries are encrypted. Nobody at the company can read them. This is not a marketing claim — it is how the system is technically designed.

## 6. What Is Still Missing or Not Perfect

We want to be honest about where the app currently stands.

**The app is early-stage.** Some things that are designed to work do not work yet. Some things work but are rough around the edges.

**Onboarding is minimal.** Right now, when you sign up, you land directly in a chat. There is no explanation of what each section does. We know this is confusing and are working on it.

**The weekly insight summaries are still basic.** The pattern data is real, but the written summaries are currently generated from templates rather than fully personalized AI writing. They can feel a bit mechanical if you use the app for several weeks. We are upgrading this.

**Elena does not always remember things perfectly.** The memory system exists but is not yet very precise. Elena may not always reference things you have shared before, or may do so at an awkward moment. This is a known limitation in the current version.

**There is a daily usage limit.** The free version of the app has a cap on how much you can chat in a day. We are still figuring out the right limits — we do not want to cut off a conversation at a meaningful moment, but we also need to manage costs. This limit may be adjusted before launch.

**Re-engagement is not fully built.** If you stop using the app for a few days, you will not receive a notification or email reminding you to come back. That feature is being built but is not live yet.
